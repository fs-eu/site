An engagement, week by week

02: How it runs
WK.01

Find the leverage point

The crew embeds and maps where your hours actually go. We pick one workflow where AI compounds: high volume, clear judgement, measurable outcome. Then we agree in writing what will be running in production by the end.

WK.02—05

Strike while embedded

Build agents in your stack, against your real data, with your engineers pairing in. Weekly demos of working automation, never slideware. Evals are written before the agent is, so we measure it honestly from day one.

WK.06

Prove it holds

Production traffic, evals and observability wired, humans in the loop exactly where they must be and nowhere else. If it doesn't hold, we stay until it does. That's in the contract, not the brochure.

WK.06+

Hand over the forge

Your team owns it: runbooks, eval suites, and the pairing hours to extend it. The first agent is the expensive one. Everything after rides on the same rails. We leave a system, not a dependency.
